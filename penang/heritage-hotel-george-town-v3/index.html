About Kingston Hotel George Town

Kingston Hotel George Town is a 3-star heritage hotel offering affordable accommodation in the heart of Penang's historic district. The property suits budget-conscious travellers, heritage enthusiasts, and those seeking authentic colonial-era surroundings without premium pricing.

Located in George Town, the hotel sits just a 2-minute walk from Armenian Street, one of the area's most significant heritage attractions. Guests are positioned within the UNESCO-listed George Town streetscape, surrounded by traditional shophouses, street art, and cultural landmarks.

Kingston Hotel George Town is an ideal base for heritage tourism in Penang. Its proximity to Armenian Street places you at the centre of George Town's most photographed and historically rich precinct. The hotel's location allows easy access to colonial architecture, traditional trades, and centuries-old temples and mosques that define the island's cultural landscape.
