About Kingston Hotel George Town

Kingston Hotel George Town is a 3-star hotel located in the heart of George Town's heritage precinct. The hotel suits travellers seeking affordable accommodation with direct access to Penang's UNESCO World Heritage zone, ideal for heritage explorers and cultural tourists on a budget.

The hotel stands just a 2-minute walk from Armenian Street, one of George Town's most significant heritage corridors lined with colonial shophouses, street art, and historical landmarks. From this central location, guests can easily explore the surrounding heritage quarter on foot.

Kingston Hotel George Town places you at the epicentre of George Town's UNESCO World Heritage precinct. Stay steps away from Armenian Street's iconic street art and colonial architecture, and spend your days discovering centuries-old shophouses, hidden temples, and the living heritage of this coastal trading port—all within walking distance from your room.
