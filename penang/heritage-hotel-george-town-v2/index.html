About Kingston Hotel George Town

Kingston Hotel George Town is a 3-star hotel in the heart of George Town's historic district, ideal for budget-conscious travelers and heritage enthusiasts seeking authentic accommodation. The hotel offers comfortable rooms and convenient amenities at affordable rates, with check-in from 3:00 PM and check-out until 12:00 PM.

Located just 2 minutes' walk from Armenian Street, Kingston Hotel places you at the gateway to George Town's most celebrated heritage area. From the hotel, you can easily explore UNESCO World Heritage shophouses, colonial architecture, and the vibrant street art that defines this historic quarter.

Explore George Town's Living Heritage

Kingston Hotel George Town positions you perfectly to experience the city's architectural and cultural heritage. Your 2-minute walk to Armenian Street connects you directly to one of George Town's most preserved heritage corridors, where 19th-century shophouses, traditional crafts, and historical landmarks showcase Penang's multicultural past. The hotel's central location makes it an ideal base for discovering Fort Cornwallis, heritage temples, and the intricate street murals that have made George Town a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
