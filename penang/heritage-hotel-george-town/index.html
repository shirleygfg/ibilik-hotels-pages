Heritage hotel in George Town Penang

Kingston Hotel George Town is a 3-star hotel offering affordable accommodation in the heart of Penang's historic district. The hotel suits heritage travellers, culture enthusiasts, and budget-conscious visitors seeking authentic access to George Town's UNESCO World Heritage streets and colonial architecture.

Located just a 2-minute walk from Armenian Street, the hotel places you at the centre of George Town's most storied thoroughfare. Armenian Street is lined with restored shophouses, art galleries, cafés, and heritage landmarks, making it the gateway to the town's preserved cultural landscape.

Kingston Hotel George Town is your base for exploring George Town's living heritage. From its convenient location near Armenian Street, you can walk directly into the UNESCO-listed old town to discover colonial-era buildings, traditional trades, street art, and the layers of multicultural history that define Penang. The hotel's central position lets you experience heritage attractions on foot without the need for transport, making it ideal for immersive cultural tourism.
