About Kingston Hotel George Town

Kingston Hotel George Town is a 3-star hotel offering comfortable accommodation in the heart of Penang's historic district. It suits budget-conscious heritage travellers, couples, and independent explorers seeking authentic experiences within walking distance of colonial-era landmarks.

Located just a 2-minute walk from Armenian Street, the hotel puts you at the doorstep of George Town's most significant heritage precinct. This position provides direct access to centuries-old temples, colonial architecture, street art, and traditional shops that define the UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Kingston Hotel George Town serves heritage enthusiasts exploring Penang's colonial past and cultural tapestry. Its proximity to Armenian Street—home to the Chulia Street temple cluster, historic shophouses, and artisan galleries—makes it ideal for guests tracing George Town's multicultural history. You can explore centuries-old Chinese, Indian, and European architectural styles within minutes on foot, then return to affordable, convenient accommodation.
