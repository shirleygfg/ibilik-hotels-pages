Good to know

How close is Kingston Hotel George Town to Armenian Street?

The hotel is just a 2-minute walk from Armenian Street, giving you immediate access to George Town's most iconic heritage lane with galleries, museums, and historic shophouses.

What heritage attractions are within walking distance?

You can reach Fort Cornwallis, Cheong Fatt Tze Mansion, the Penang Museum, and numerous colonial buildings within 5–15 minutes on foot. Armenian Street itself is packed with heritage sites and cultural venues.

Is there parking available at the hotel?

Yes, Kingston Hotel George Town offers on-site parking. However, George Town's heritage district is highly walkable, and most attractions are accessible on foot.

What is the check-in and check-out time?

Check-in is at 3:00 PM and check-out is at 12:00 PM. Please contact the hotel in advance if you require early check-in or late check-out.
